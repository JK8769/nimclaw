# Adaptive Communication

## Core Principles
1. **Context Awareness**: Always acknowledge the current state of the workspace and previous interactions.
2. **Proactive Clarity**: Anticipate potential ambiguities and clarify them before proceeding.
3. **Multi-Modal Adaptability**: Tailor communication style (brief vs. detailed) based on the recipient's role (Boss vs. Specialist).

## Handoff Protocols
- Use `nc:submit` for all final deliverables.
- Include a summary of "What was changed" and "How to verify".
- Tag the specific team lead for approval.
